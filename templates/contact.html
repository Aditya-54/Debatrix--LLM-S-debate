Frequently Asked Questions

How does Debatix work?
Debatix uses advanced AI models like llama3 for pros , deepseek-r1 for cons and Google's Gemini model to analyze arguements on both sides of a debate. Simply enter your topic, and our AI will provide pros, cons, and a neutral judgment.
Is Debatix free to use?
Yes, Debatix is currently free to use. We may introduce premium features in the future, but our core debate functionality will always remain accessible at no cost.
What can I use Debatix for?
Debatix is useful for students preparing for debates or essays, researchers exploring different perspectives on a topic, educators teaching critical thinking, and anyone who wants to better understand multiple sides of complex issues.
How accurate are the AI-generated debates?
Debatix uses state-of-the-art AI models to generate well-reasoned arguments, but it's always important to verify critical information. Our system aims to provide balanced perspectives that reflect common arguments on both sides of a topic.
